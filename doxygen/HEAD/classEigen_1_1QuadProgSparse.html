Detailed Description

Sparse quadratic program.

\begin{align} \underset{{x} \in \mathbb{R}^n}{\text{minimize}} & \ \frac{1}{2}{x^TQx} + {c^Tx} \nonumber \\ \text{subject to} & \ A_{eq} x = b_{eq} \\ & \ A_{ineq} x \leq b_{ineq} \end{align}
